During the course of working with our clients,  it has come to light that many, who are large energy users and actively considering implementing Combined Heat and Power (CHP) or Co-generation, but are not fully familiar with  energy contracting , particularly gas, steam electricity  purchasing commercial arrangements and the associated contracts.

 

 

Energy Innovation provide support services to select and monitor gas suppliers and implement commercial arrangements for  energy purchasing.

 

Energy Innovation are keen advocates for gas trading at an Irish Title Transfer Facility sometimes called a virtual balancing point.

 

This is a virtual transfer point where parties have the  opportunity to sell or purchase

( i.e.  transfer title)  gas that is already in gas  system ( i.e. ‘entry-paid gas’).
